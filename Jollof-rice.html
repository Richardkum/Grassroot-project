Jollof-rice with grilled chicken



Ingredients needed for Jollof-Rice-With-Chicken:

  • Rice
  • spices
  • tomatoes and pepper
  • chicken
  • onion, garlic and ginger
  • carrots, other vegetables
  • water
  • tomato paste
  • Salt

Steps for making Jollof-Rice-With-Chicken

  1. Blend your onion,pepper,garlic
  2. Put your cooking pot on fire and add oil
  3. Add your blended ingredients to the hot oil
  4. Add your tomato paste and stir
  5. add your spices and salt to taste
  6. When the stew is cooked, wash your rice and add it
  7. Add water and cover it to cook
  8. Spice your chicken and grill it
